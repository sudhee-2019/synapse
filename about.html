About Synapse 2019

"Knowledge is power, but enthusiasm pulls the switch." This monsoon, SYNAPSE 2k19 is here to fill you with tech fervour!

Synapse is a National level technical symposium conducted by department of ECE as a part of CBIT's tech fest - SUDHEE. This year, we're super excited to bring forth a wide range of tech-fun events ranging from paper presentations to tech expos to mock interviews and many more fun activities. The train is all set, it's time to embark a journey filled with knowledge and loads of fun!!

Synapse 2018 saw unprecedented success with a footfall of 200 for events, and total cash prizes worth 15000, Synapse 2019 is back again to continue that legacy!
